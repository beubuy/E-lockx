Tools for Enhanced Security

Discover trusted software and browser extensions that can significantly boost your online safety. LockX recommends these for consideration.

Password Manager (e.g., LastPass)

Securely store and generate strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.

Visit LastPass

Privacy Browser Extension (e.g., Privacy Badger)

Automatically blocks invisible trackers, ensuring your browsing remains private.

Visit Privacy Badger

Encryption Software (e.g., VeraCrypt)
